David Attenborough

English broadcaster, biologist, natural historian and author

David Attenborough is a revered British broadcaster and natural historian. Known for his captivating documentaries, he has dedicated his life to showcasing the wonders of the natural world. With a soothing voice and infectious passion, Attenborough has inspired millions to appreciate and protect our planet, becoming an environmental icon.

Three Biggest Achievements...

  1. Iconic nature documentaries that captivate global audiences.
  2. Inspiring millions to appreciate and protect the environment.
  3. Becoming a revered environmental icon and advocate.

3 Favourite Animals...

  • Gorillas: Attenborough has showcased the intelligence and social behavior of gorillas in his documentaries, highlighting their similarities to humans.
  • Birds of Paradise: He has a fascination for the elaborate courtship displays and vibrant plumage of these exotic birds found in the rainforests of Papua New Guinea.
  • Blue Whales: As the largest animals on Earth, Attenborough has featured blue whales in his work, emphasizing their majestic presence and the need to protect their marine habitats.
